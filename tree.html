Reading phylogenies

Three mistakes to avoid

A family tree is a hypothesis about relationships. It does not rank animals from “lower” to “higher,” and the order of tips can rotate without changing the relationships.

01

Humans did not evolve from chimpanzees

Both lineages descend from an extinct common ancestor and have changed independently since their split.

02

Fossils are rarely proven ancestors

A fossil taxon may sit close to an ancestral population while still representing a side branch.

03

Branch length here is simplified

Use the timeline for chronological scale; this view prioritizes relationships and readability.
